Markus Prock ( born 22 June 1964 ) is an Austria n luge r who competed between 1983 and 2002 . Born in Innsbruck , Prock competed in six winning three medals in the men 's singles event with two silvers ( 1992 , 1994 ) and one bronze ( 2002 ) . At the FIL World Luge Championships , Prock won 13 medals , including five gold ( Men 's singles : 1987 , 1996 ; Mixed team : 1996 , 1997 , 1999 ) , four silvers ( Men 's singles : 1990 , 1997 ; Mixed team : 1991 , 1993 ) , and four bronzes ( Men 's singles : 1995 , 2001 ; Mixed team : 1995 , 2001 ) . Prock won six medals at the FIL European Luge Championships with five in men 's singles ( gold : 1994 , 1998 , 2002 ; silver : 1988 , 1990 ) and one medal in the mixed team event ( bronze : 2002 ) . He also won the overall ten times in men 's singles ( 1987-8 , 1990-1 , 1991-2 , 1992-3 , 1993-4 , 1994-5 , 1995-6 , 1996-7 , 1998-9 , 2001-2 ) . As of 2007 , he was the sports director of the Tyrolean luge association . He also manages his nephew , Gregor Schlierenzauer , who was second in the 2006-07 entering the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2007 in Sapporo , Japan
