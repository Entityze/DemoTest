Steep Falls is a census-designated place ( CDP ) in the town of Standish in Cumberland County , Maine , United States . The population was 1,139 at the 2010 census . It is part of the Portland -- South Portland -- Biddeford , Maine . Steep Falls is located in the western corner of the town of Standish along the Saco River at . Maine State Route 11 crosses the Saco River into the community , heading southwest towards Limington and Limerick and north towards Naples . Maine State Route 113 coincides with Route 11 through the center of Steep Falls , then heads southeast towards Standish village and northwest towards Hiram and Fryeburg .
