The 2009 Team Speedway Junior European Championship will be the 2nd UEM Team Speedway Junior European Championship season . The Final will be held on August 23 , 2009 in Holsted , Denmark . The championship was won by Poland ( 40 points ) , who they beat defending champion Sweden ( 38 points ) , host team Denmark ( 26 points ) and Czech Republic ( 15 points ) In the Final will be the defending Champions Sweden , host team Denmark ( 3rd place in 2008 Final ) and Poland ( 4th place ) . A last finalist will be determined in one Semi-Final . In Bockhorn , Germany on 18 July will be , Czech Republic , Russia and host team Germany ( 2nd place ) . The Russian team withdrew and was replaced by Finland
