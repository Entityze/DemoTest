It is smaller and lighter in comparison to other general laptops or notebooks . It is like a standard normal book which could be easily carried with the hand . Since it is so portable , an individual could bring it to wherever he or she wants to travel to . It could be light in the bag and there more spaces in the bag where other things could also be stuffed in . Ladies will especially appreciate this as she may have keys , make-up things , tissues , mobile phone and water bottle to carry together .
