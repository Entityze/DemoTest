Two days after the West Virginia Democrat appeared to deal the For the People Act a death blow by announcing his opposition to it in an op-ed, frustration over the inaction in Congress on voting rights continued to bubble around the county. State legislators in Arizona are calling on Sen. Kyrsten Sinema (D) to show more leadership on the issue, and Texas lawmakers are preparing to press Democratic leaders to show the same commitment to voting rights that Democrats in the state House demonstrated there last month, when they staged a walkout that scuttled a piece of voting legislation.
