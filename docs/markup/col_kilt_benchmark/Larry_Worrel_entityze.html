Lawrence ' Larry ' Roosevelt Worrell ( born 28 August 1943 in Saint Thomas , Barbados ) is a Barbadian born former English . Worrell was a right-handed who bowled right-arm off break . Worrell made his first-class debut for Hampshire , playing a single match in 1969 against the touring New Zealanders . During the 1969 season Worrell also represented Dorset in the , playing against Berkshire , Cornwall and Somerset Second XI . In 1971 Worrell returned to Hampshire for his second spell at the club . Worrell made his County Championship debut against Nottinghamshire . Worrell played 32 first-class matches for Hampshire , the last of which came against Leicestershire in 1972 .
