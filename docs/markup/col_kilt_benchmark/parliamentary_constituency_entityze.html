Zielona Góra is a Polish that is coterminous with the Lubusz Voivodeship . It elects twelve members of the Sejm and three members of the Senate . The district has the number ' 8 ' and is named after the city of Zielona Góra . It includes the counties of Gorzów , Krosno Odrzańskie , Międzyrzecz , Nowa Sól , Słubice , Strzelce-Drezdenko , Sulęcin , Świebodzin , Wschowa , Żagań , Żary , and Zielona Góra and the of Gorzów Wielkopolski
