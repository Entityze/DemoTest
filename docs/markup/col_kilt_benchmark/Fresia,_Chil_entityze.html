Fresia is a and commune in Llanquihue Province , Los Lagos Region , Chile . According to the 2002 census of the National Statistics Institute , Fresia spans an area of and has 12,804 inhabitants ( 6,580 men and 6,224 women ) . Of these , 6,144 ( 48 % ) lived in urban area s and 6,660 ( 52 % ) in rural area s . The population fell by 1.6 % ( 209 persons ) between the 1992 and 2002 censuses . As a commune , Fresia is a third-level administered by a municipal council , headed by an alcalde who is directly elected every four years . The 2008-2012 alcalde is Bernardo Espinoza Villalobos ( PS ) .
