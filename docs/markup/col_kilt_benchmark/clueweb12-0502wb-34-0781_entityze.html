Packed within every picture is a collection of pretty pixels varying in shades of red , green and blue . Everytime an Aminus3 photoblogger uploads an image , our crack team of palette pondering robot scientists use our patent pending three pass scan technique to create a magical color scheme for all to enjoy . Below are some of the popular images that contain the color #AD9 ( #AADD99 ) or a close match to it . On a scale from 0 to 255 , this color contains 170 red , 221 green and 153 blue .
