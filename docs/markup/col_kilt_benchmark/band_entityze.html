Diabolique is a Swedish gothic metal band , formed in 1995 after the dissolution of Liers in Wait . The group is heavily influenced by The Sisters of Mercy , Black Sabbath , and Fields of the Nephilim , and in sound are similar to the styles of Charon , Tiamat , Moonspell , and Type O Negative . The band 's current and former line-up has consisted of many musicians from progenital Gothenburg melodic death metal bands ; Kristian Wåhlin , Johan Osterberg , and Alf Svensson were in Grotesque ( a predecessor of At the Gates , who Alf would eventually join ) ; and Daniel Svensson briefly played drums with the group before going on to major success with In Flames . The band 's most recent release was The Green Goddess ( 2001 ) . Various sources differ as to band 's status .
