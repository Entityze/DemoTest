Some consider it as a philosophy , and others consider it as a pragmatic methodology . Before open source became widely adopted , developers and producers used a variety of phrases to describe the concept ; the term open source gained popularity with the rise of the Internet and its enabling of diverse production models , communication paths , and interactive communities . Subsequently , open source software became the most prominent face of open source .
