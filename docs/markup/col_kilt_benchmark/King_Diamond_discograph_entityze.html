The discography of King Diamond , a Danish heavy metal band , consists of twelve studio releases , two live albums , five compilations , six singles , and four music video s. King Diamond was formed in 1985 , after the dissolution of the group Mercyful Fate , by vocalist King Diamond , guitarists Andy LaRocque and Michael Denner , bassist Timi Hansen , and drummer Mikkey Dee . The following year , the band released their debut album Fatal Portrait , which charted at number 33 in Sweden . King Diamond 's second studio album , Abigail , was released on October 21 , 1987 , and reached number 123 in the US , number 39 in Sweden and number 68 in the Netherlands . Following some line-up changes , the group released the album " Them " in 1988 , which peaked at number 38 in Sweden , number 65 in the Netherlands , and at number 89 in the US , making " Them " King Diamond 's highest charting album in North America . The following year , the band released the follow-up album Conspiracy , which charted at number 111 in North America , number 41 in Sweden and at number 64 in the Netherlands . In 1990 , after more line-up changes , King Diamond released the album The Eye , which only charted at number 179 in the US , which makes The Eye King Diamond 's lowest charting album in North America . After Mercyful Fate was reformed in 1993 , King Diamond remained inactive until 1995 , when the band released the album The Spider 's Lullabye with the line-up of King Diamond , Andy LaRocque , guitarist Herb Simonsen , bassist Chris Estes and drummer Darrin Anthony . The album went on to reach number 31 in Finland . The Spider 's Lullabye was followed by The Graveyard ( # 23 in Finland ) and Voodoo ( # 27 in Finland , # 55 in the Netherlands ) in 1996 and 1998 respectively . In 2000 , King Diamond released the album House of God , which peaked at number 60 in Sweden .
