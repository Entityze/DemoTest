Daniel Bovet ( 23 March 1907 -- 8 April 1992 ) was a Swiss-born Italian pharmacologist who won the 1957 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for his discovery of drugs that block the actions of specific neurotransmitter s . His other research included work on chemotherapy , sulfa drug s , the sympathetic nervous system , the pharmacology of curare , and other neuropharmacological interests . In 1965 , Bovet led a study team which concluded that smoking of tobacco cigarettes increased users ' intelligence .
