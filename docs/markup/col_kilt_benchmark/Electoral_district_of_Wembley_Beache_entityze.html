The Electoral district of Wembley Beaches was a Legislative Assembly electorate in the state of Western Australia . The district was named for the inner western suburb of Wembley , which fell within its borders . Wembley Beaches came into existence at the 1950 election , and was largely created from the northern and coastal sections of the Leederville , which had grown considerably in population in the years immediately following World War II . The seat included the inner suburbs of Floreat Park , Jolimont and Wembley , and areas further north including Doubleview , Gwelup , Innaloo , North Beach , Scarborough , Trigg and Watermans Bay . While reasonably safe for the Liberal Party , Wembley Beaches was won for a term by the Labor , who were relatively strong in the Scarborough and Innaloo region throughout the seat 's existence . The seat was abolished ahead of the 1962 election , with its area being split between the seats of Wembley and Karrinyup .
